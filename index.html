OUR MISSION

A bold movement investing in youth to tackle inequality and provide an opportunity for youth to upskill themselves.

Since 2005, Life Choices has impacted over 200,000 lives with CHOICES and NOT CHARITY.

OUR PHILOSOPHY


Life Choices believes that the human spirit is remarkably resilient – no matter who our parents are, where we live, or what kinds of connections, abilities, and opportunities life may have offered us. Even in circumstances where the odds seem hopelessly stacked against us – there is endless potential waiting to be freed.

WE ALL HAVE THE POTENTIAL TO SHAPE OUR LIVES IN WAYS THAT WILL BRING US THE MEANING AND SUCCESS WE LONG FOR.

OUR WORK

We understand that no two people are the same, which means our interactive and constantly evolving interventions are designed to be holistic and to meet the needs of the individual. We believe in transformation from inside out. This, in turn, creates a positive ripple-effect in the community.
